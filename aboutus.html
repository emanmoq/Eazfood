We love local businesses.

Founded in 2020 in Chatham, Ontario, we’re a food ordering and delivery app that allows customers to choose their favorite meal from a list of restaurants; and place their order for delivery or pick-up. EzeFood provides customers and restaurant owners with an easy and convenient food ordering and delivery experience.

Our Timeline

2019

The Idea

A few local guys meet in Chatham, Ontario and decide to build a better way to support local business with food delivery. After spending years in the food delivery business, they realized there has to be a better way.

2020

Launched in a Pandemic

By Jan 1st of 2020 we secure our first restaurant and shortly after COVID-19 shut down everything. We fought our way through and by December of 2020, we had 4 cities and 100 restaurants and over 20,000 ordrers processed

2021

Expansion

With a focus on expanding, by March we had successfully launched in 7 cities. Within the first 5 months of the year we have over 150 restaurants and over 20,000 orders processed on our platform.
